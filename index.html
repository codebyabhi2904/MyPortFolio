Services

Web Design

A web designer creates visually appealing and user-friendly website layouts, focusing on design elements like color schemes, typography, and graphics. They use tools like Adobe Creative Suite and Figma to craft responsive designs for various devices. Web designers collaborate with developers to implement their designs and often work on UI/UX to enhance usability. They need a mix of creative and technical skills, including knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Web Development

Web development involves building and maintaining websites, focusing on functionality and performance. Web developers use languages like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and server-side languages such as PHP, Python, and Ruby to create dynamic and interactive sites. They work on both front-end (user interface) and back-end (server-side logic) development. Collaboration with web designers is essential to integrate visual designs into fully functional websites.

Shopify Development

Shopify theme development involves creating and customizing themes for Shopify stores. Developers use HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Liquid (Shopify's templating language) to design visually appealing and responsive themes. They ensure the themes are user-friendly and optimized for performance. Collaboration with designers and store owners is crucial to align the theme with brand aesthetics and functional requirements.
